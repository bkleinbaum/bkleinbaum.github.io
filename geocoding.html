Finding Council Members

A school sought to gain support for its budget requests from the City Council. It asked Ben to find who each student's Council Member, State Assembly Member and State Senator were to show them how money they allocated helped their constiuents. Ben geocoded more than a 1000 students' addresses to determine their legislators and provided the school with a spreadsheet and map that it could show to elected officials.
